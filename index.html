Case

Willab garden

Buying a greenhouse is a big decision. Willab Garden has always made it feel less so. The new visual identity brought that same warmth online — a colour palette rooted in nature, a tone that guides rather than pushes, and a shopping experience that feels like talking to someone who actually knows their stuff.

About me

I don't conjure ideas from thin air — I solve problems. Making something difficult simpler and more beautiful is what drives me. Finding the right tone, the right feel, the interface that just works — that's my thing.

I'm a Senior Product Designer at Valtech, based in Gothenburg, with close to 30 years in the digital industry. Enough experience to know what works, still curious enough to question it.

My work spans branding, design systems, prototyping and user research — sometimes all in the same project. Lately I've also been exploring what AI tools bring to the design process.

I believe the best ideas come from real collaboration — where one idea sparks the next and we build something together neither of us would have made alone. I value spaces where people feel free to speak their minds.
